Recipe of Chicken Tandoori

Chicken Tandoori is a traditional punjabi cooking recipe that makes all peoples finger-lick actions

    Tandoori Chicken
  1. Get a 1 kg raw Chicken
  2. Boil it atleast 20 min for removing impurities
  3. add some stuff like tomato pury ,ginger
  4. add 1 litre of water
  5. add some salt as you like
  6. mix well
  7. and your chicken is ready
